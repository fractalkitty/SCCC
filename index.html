Salem Creative Coding Club

At Salem Creative Coding Club, we explore the intersection of art and technology. Through coding, we create interactive digital experiences, generative artwork, and unique creative projects. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just curious about coding, this is a space to experiment, collaborate, and express your creativity through code.

Our focus is on using coding languages such as JavaScript, Python, p5.js, Processing, and others to build visually stunning and conceptually interesting projects. It's all about pushing boundaries, learning, and having fun with code!

Every Third Friday at 7pm!

For 2024 we are online as we get established, but plan to start meeting in person come 2025.

Monthly Prompts

Each month, we present a new creative prompt to inspire your next project. Even though we have prompts, you’re always welcome to bring your own ideas and projects to work on. This is everyone’s space to code creatively.

Join Us!

We welcome people from all backgrounds and skill levels, whether you're a professional developer, a designer, an artist, or completely new to coding. The only requirement is an open mind and a desire to create something new!

To become a member, simply sign up on our sign-up page/link. We look forward to seeing you there and creating amazing things together!
